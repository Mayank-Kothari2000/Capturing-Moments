List of photography awards

This list of photography awards is an index to articles that describe notable awards given for photography. It does not include photojournalism, which is covered in the list of journalism awards. The list is organized by the region and country of the organization that gives the award, but some awards are open to international competitors.This is a dynamic list and may never be able to satisfy particular standards for completeness. You can help by adding missing items with reliable sources.
